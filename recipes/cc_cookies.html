Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

Description

If you're looking up this recipe, you've probably heard of chocolate chip cookies before. This classic recipes is sure to be a great addition to literally any occasion. After all, nobody can be sad while eating a nice, warm cookie!

This recipe will walk you through the basic steps of creating the world famous cookie from scratch. Great for even new bakers!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup softened butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

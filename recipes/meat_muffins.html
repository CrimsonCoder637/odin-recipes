Meat Muffins Recipe

Description

In traditional Mennonite fashion, this dish can be thrown together quickly with ingredients that almost any Anglo family is guaranteed to have on hand. It's also tasty enough to keep you coming back!

A very simple recipe for even those unfamiliar with cooking. Simply combine your ground beef, can of biscuit rolls, barbecue sauce, and cheese to create this delicious, savory dish that will keep you coming back!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 can of biscuit rolls
  • 3/4 cup of barbecue sauce
  • 2 tbl of brown sugar (optional)
  • 1 cup of shredded cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  2. Cook the ground beef in a skillet until browned throughout. Add in barbecue sauce and optional brown sugar when beef is nearly cooked.
  3. Line a muffin tin with the triangles of biscuit dough from the can of biscuit rolls. Ensure that the dough completely fills each slot in the muffin tin.
  4. Fill each dough slot in the muffin tin with a spoonful of cooked ground beef.
  5. Cover each muffin with shredded cheese.
  6. Place the muffin tin in the oven for 18 minutes.
  7. Remove muffin tin when muffins begin to brown on top. Serve with colorful vegetables for a varietous meal!
